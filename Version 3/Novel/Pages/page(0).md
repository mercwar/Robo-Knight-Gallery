## 📖 **ROBO-KNIGHT: ASCENT OF THE LION**  

### **Chapter 3 — The Throne That Waits**

They entered the hall in silence.

The stone beneath their boots remembered every war.  
The banners above them bore the twin lions — not as decoration, but as law.

The **Laser Knight** stepped forward.  
His sword hummed. His shield pulsed. His cybernetic arm flexed beneath the plating.

To his left, **Synbot** smiled.  
To his right, the **Red Knight** and **Black Knight** held formation.

The **Cyborg Sentinel** scanned the shadows.  
The **Desert Knight** stood tall, spear gleaming.

And at the far end — the **Throne**.  
Occupied. Watching.

The figure seated there wore armor older than memory.  
Two horned guards flanked him, silent, holding banners.

The Covenant had summoned them.  
But the throne had not spoken.

Not yet.
