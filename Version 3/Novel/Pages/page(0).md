

## ⚔️ **THE ROBO-KNIGHT COVENANT** (Version B.4 Alignment)

> **“We are the armored memory of a broken world.  
> We do not forget. We do not drift. We do not kneel.”**

### 🔐 Core Protocols
- **Lion Emblem**: Only worn by oath-bound knights. Unauthorized use triggers ceremonial purge.
- **Shield Law**: Every shield must bear the twin lions or be ritually destroyed.
- **Sword Protocol**: Laser swords must be raised only in defense of the Covenant or during ceremonial merge.
- **Synbot Clause**: Synbot’s smile is the signal of peace. If corrupted, the merge is void.
- **Plume Order**: Red = Honor, Gold = Command, Black = Memory, White = Ascension.

## 📖 **ROBO-KNIGHT: ASCENT OF THE LION**  
### **Book I — The Armor That Remembers**

### **Chapter 2 — The Shoreline Merge**

The sand hissed beneath their boots.  
Seven figures stood in formation — metal, memory, and myth.

At the center, the Laser Knight raised his blade.  
Blue light split the sky. His shield pulsed with twin lions, and his cybernetic arm gleamed beneath the plating.

To his left, Synbot smiled — a straight line, slightly curved.  
The signal was clear: peace was possible.

The Cyborg Sentinel stood beside him, red eye scanning the horizon.  
He had no allegiance. Only purpose.

To the right, the Red Knight gripped his sword.  
The Black Knight held the line.  
The Desert Knight stood tall, spear gleaming, wings folded.

They had never fought together.  
They had never spoken.

But the Covenant had summoned them.  
And the shoreline had accepted their merge.

Above them, the clouds parted.  
The sky watched.  
The war waited.
